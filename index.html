Our history

Founded in 1982 by Tony Lorenzo, a *passionate automotive enthusiast* and entrepreneur, Vortex Dealership Company began as a small but ambitious car lot in the heart of Miami. Tony, a Cuban-American visionary, recognized the city’s growing demand for reliable and stylish vehicles and set out to create a dealership that offered more than just cars — it offered trust, transparency, and top-tier service.

Starting with just a handful of used vehicles on SW 13th Street, Tony built Vortex on the values of hard work, community, and customer satisfaction. Word spread quickly, and by the late 1980s, Vortex had grown into one of Miami’s most respected independent dealerships. Tony’s *hands-on approach* and personal connection with every customer became a trademark of the Vortex experience.

Through the decades, Vortex expanded to multiple locations, including Brickell Bay Dr and SW 1st Avenue, becoming a trusted name across South Florida. Today, Vortex Dealership Company continues to thrive as a legacy-driven brand, still rooted in the principles Tony Lorenzo established over 40 years ago — driven by passion, powered by people.
